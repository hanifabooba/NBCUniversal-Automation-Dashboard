Our story

We began in a tiny backyard greenhouse, growing enough to put healthy food on our own dining table, working side-by-side with our kids. From those first rows to the early field crew, we kept our focus on clean, chemical-free produce. Today, NBCuniversal delivers consistent organic harvests while training new growers and expanding sustainable irrigation so more families can eat well.

Every crop is tracked by our managers and logged in the same dashboards you see across the app, keeping quality and timelines visible to the entire team.
